How to use

ForceOP/AuthMeCracker

in the Wurst Client

Before you start, make sure the server is cracked and has AuthMe or a similar PlugIn.


More info

This step is important!

Do not ignore it!

Now collect the usernames of as many admins as you can.

The more usernames you have, the higher your chance of getting OP.

Add them to your alt list so you can easily use them on the server.

The easiest way to do this is by typing .addalt <username> while you are on the server.

Once you have them all in your alt list, log in as one of them and join the server.

You will notice that you can't do anything yet, because you don't yet have the AuthMe password.

To get the password, turn ForceOP/AuthMeCracker on, press the Start button and wait until it's done.

This might not succeed right in the first try. That's why you collected several usernames. Just repeat this process with the other usernames until you find one that works.

Once you're done with that, you can either give OP to your own account or grief the server as the admin.

Happy griefing!
